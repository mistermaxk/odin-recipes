Linguini with White Clam Sauce

Description

An Italian classic dinner you can prepare in under thirty minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 6oz cans of clams
  • 1/4 cup of white onion or shallot, minced
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 6-8 cremini or portabello mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 6 black or kalamata olives, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon oregano, chopped or dry
  • 1 tablespoon basil, chopped or dry
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice, or 1 half lemon, juiced
  • 1/4 cup white coooking wine

Steps

  1. On medium heat, melt butter and heat oil in medium pan.
  2. Add onions and cook until clear, two to three minutes.
  3. Add olives, garlic, mushrooms, and clam juice seperated from canned clams, and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat, add herbs, spices, and remaining liquids. Stir and let simmer two to five minutes.
  5. Add bell pepper and cook for ten minutes until liquid volume reduces and thickens.
  6. Remove pan from heat and stir in clams.
  7. Serve over freshly cooked linguini or pasta of your choice.
